2020 Democratic Presidential Candidates
Voter Choice Percentages
Joe Biden, Bernie Sanders, and Elizabeth Warren are the current top three choices for Democratic primary voters
Favorability Percentages
Biden, Sanders, and Warren are also the most favorable candidates, with voters having no recognition or opinion of the majority of other candidates.
State Ties
Candidates have ties to states around the country, with a large proportion having experience working in the Northeastern US, Texas, and California. (The map displays the electoral college results for the 2016 presidential election)
Background/Experience
Regarding experience, the vast majority of candidates have political backgrounds, followed by those with entrepreneurial, legal, and military expertise.
Stances on Critical Issues
Despite sharing many core values, candidates lie on a wide spectrum of opinions with regard to solving the country's most critical issues.
Key Criticisms
All candidates have faced criticism for past stances or conduct, inexperience/ anonymity, etc. all of which will play a vital role in final voter selection
